Final Project Brief

I plan on updating the Game On project by updating its visual design, and by developing its functionality to have a more enjoyable gameplay experience.

In terms of visual updates, a lot more can be done with CSS animations and I want to incorporate more of that into the project. I also want to try drawing more things to put into the game. In terms of gameplay, I want to both balance out the difficulty throughout the game and add progression elements, such as a shop from which the player can choose upgrades in between rounds. I also want to explore more interactive ways to execute attacks (like playing a brief minigame to determine success).

I would love to have feedback from both players who are experienced in the turn-based RPG genre and from users who might be unfamiliar. For the visuals I should also get feedback from designers.
